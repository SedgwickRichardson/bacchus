Bacchus is a social wine app that allows you to capture, save and share your wine experiences with friends.

You can create and save tasting notes, find out where to buy the wines you like, connect with, and plan trips to, wineries.

While there is an increase in wine consumption, there is also a growing number of consumers that want to connect directly with the producers of wine. They also want to share their views on wine, creating greater democracy in the wine experience where comments and ratings are driven by a global wine community rather than a few commentators. They also want to buy wine in the most efficient and cost effective way.

Drinking wine is a social experience.

Consumers want to understand the terroir and the winemaking process and wine tourism is on the increase.

Follow wineries

Connect with the winemakers and track the progress of this years production and recommendations on which previous vintages to drink now. Plan trips to the major wine producing regions and top wineries of the world.

Not just labels

Capture, save and share wine drinking moments with your friends. Create, save and share tasting notes. Locate the wines you want to buy.

Follow friends and experts

Connect with friends, wine experts, celebrity users and the wine producers and see what they are drinking and why.

Connect directly with wine producers

Exclusive content provided by the top wineries in the world connects your drinking experience to the terroir and winemakers enriching the experience and building knowledge by the glass

This app gives wine producers an additional channel to promote their wine. If they have no in-line presence we can provide the tools to quickly get them there. If there is an established on-line presence we provide a complimentary channel to the global market. With very little input of time producers can dynamically connect directly with their consumers with updates on new and old vintages, special events, commentary by winemakers. Text, pictures and video clips will all be supported.

The analytics produced by the consumer activity will allow producers to monitor macro trends in consumption, specific interest in their wines and comments on visits to their cellar door. This will help them to better leverage their distribution network and create opportunities to penetrate new markets.
