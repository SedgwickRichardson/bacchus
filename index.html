Bacchus is a social wine app that allows you to keep track of the wines you’re drinking and connect directly with the wineries that create them.

Drinking wine is a social experience. So is this app.

Follow your friends, celebrity users and wineries and gain insight into their ‘wine life’; what they’re drinking, where and with whom!
